Our C.O.M.P.L.E.T.E. Values

These are the principles that guide every single thing we do. It's who we are.

Compassion

Inspiring teens to love, care, and serve to make a real difference.

Outreach

Mobilizing youth-driven movements that spread hope, share Christ’s love, and create lasting change across communities.

Mission

Equipping teens to reach others with the Healing to the Nations message through watch-parties, personal testimonies, and creative engagement.

Partnership

Joining forces with teens, parents, schools, and sponsors to expand the gospel’s reach and empower a global youth movement.

Learning

A united call to raise teen heralds who carry the gospel everywhere, turning vision into tangible kingdom expansion.

Engagement

Building bridges across cultures and communities, showing unity, love, and shared purpose through meaningful connections.

Teamwork
